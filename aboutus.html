                                      Hummingbird Design Studio

Hummingbird Design Studio has been in the making for years by Joy and Nikole Dixon. Joy and Nikole are an artistic mother-and-daughter team have been making and selling artwork and handmade items for years. In 2021 we decided we needed a place to work outside of our home, and we wanted to move closer to other family members. We were lucky to find a mini farm with about 6 acres, a home, and a business building.

We purchased the place and worked hard to set the business up.

We can offer Direct-to-Garment printing, Dye Sublimation printing, Screen Printing, and Machine Embroidering. We design logos, images for shirts, product labels, and more. We teach classes for interested individuals in resin casting, lathe work, knitting, crocheting, and candy making.

The business building has a wood shop, a printing shop, and a store o allow us to sell our creations. In the storefront, we offer our products and consignment work from very talented makers and crafters. Visit us to see our offerings at 560 Fairview Road.
